If you don't see what you need here (either in terms of data, graphics or labeling) or want to suggest some changes please get in touch with Tom Pearson, Steve Bernard or Alan Smith.

Poll of polls

These charts are of use when discussing, in general, the most recent polls. Because they take into account the seven most recent polls they are less prone to the uncertainty inherent in individual polls.

Polls over time

These charts are useful when discussing trends in polling over a particular period of time (probably not less a than a month and not more than a year or two); make sure you choose a width appropriate to the time span you're showing nothing over a year or so should be shown in the small format.
